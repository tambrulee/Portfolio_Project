About

Rising Up Ministries is dedicated to sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ through evangelism and outreach. Inspired by Mark 8:27-28, we seek to lead people into a deeper relationship with Christ by bringing His message of love, grace, and salvation to communities near and far. Through street ministry, discipleship programs, and acts of service, we strive to be the hands and feet of Jesus, reaching those in need and uplifting hearts with the hope of the Gospel. Our mission is simple: to proclaim His name, make disciples, and see lives transformed by the power of God.
